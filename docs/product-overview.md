# RegenX Product Overview

RegenX is a tokenised infrastructure platform built on Stellar, enabling wholesale investors to access clean energy assets such as solar and battery storage.

## Problem

Mid-scale infrastructure projects struggle to access capital due to:
- high minimum investment thresholds
- complex deal structuring
- limited liquidity

At the same time, investors seek infrastructure-style returns but lack direct access.

## Solution

RegenX structures real-world assets into investable products and represents them as Stellar-issued assets.

The platform enables:
- fractional ownership of infrastructure
- direct investor participation
- transparent transaction flows
- on-chain settlement and auditability

## How It Works

1. Projects are onboarded and structured into SPV or series-based investment vehicles  
2. Assets are issued on Stellar using issuer and distributor wallets  
3. Investors are onboarded and provisioned wallets  
4. Investors establish trustlines and participate in funding rounds  
5. Transactions are executed on Stellar and recorded on-chain  
6. Revenue distributions are processed using stablecoin settlement (AUDD)

## Target Users

- Wholesale and accredited investors  
- Wealth managers and advisors  
- Clean energy project developers  

## Current Focus

RegenX is currently focused on:
- solar and battery storage infrastructure  
- pilot project execution  
- transitioning from testnet to mainnet  
- enabling real-world capital deployment on Stellar  

## Vision

RegenX aims to become the infrastructure layer for tokenised real-world assets, connecting capital markets with physical infrastructure through blockchain-based settlement.
